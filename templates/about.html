Safe Routes

An approach to discover how safe it is to walk in the streets of the UK using Open Data.

Find a route and discover how safe it is

Safe Routes uses Google Maps and Open Crime Data to recommend up to three routes to walk between two locations in England, Wales and Northern Ireland, highlighting the shortest, the safest and the best (in terms of distance and crime occurrences) route.

Visualize street-level crimes along a route

Safe Routes provides a visulization of all the crime incidences that have occurred along a given route. You can identify number of incidences classified by type, and compare the different routes recommended by the application to find out the best one for you. Moreover, you can explore the details of each crime identified such as the status and outcome.

Crime data provenance

The crime data used on this web application is published for free by the Home Office of the UK. It collects the crime incidences of the 43 geographic police forces in England and Wales, the British Transport Police, the Police Service of Northern Ireland and the Ministry of Justice. For more information please visit the data.police.uk website.
